(2022 - 2025) Urban Transitions and Educational Maturity

Urban Transitions and Educational Maturity

Multiple Schools - Pyin Oo Lwin & Myitkyina

The year 2022, when I was 16 years old, marked a significant turning point in my life. Seeking a broader and more diverse educational environment, I made a definitive move to Pyin Oo Lwin to attend Grade 10. This transition to a new city was not just a change of location, but an opportunity to broaden my academic horizons. Subsequently, I returned to my hometown, Myitkyina, where I completed my senior high school years. I attended No. 9 Basic Education High School (BEHS-9, Myitkyina) for Grade 11 and pursued my final year, Grade 12, at Shwe Pyi Thit Private High School. The experience of moving between different cities and schools during these formative years has been invaluable. It has gifted me with the ability to adapt quickly to new environments and the essential skill of communicating effectively across diverse social circles. These transitions have fostered a sense of resilience and maturity that I carry with me into my current university life at UCSY.
